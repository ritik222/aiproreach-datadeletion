User Data Deletion Policy

At AIProReach, we take privacy seriously. If you request the deletion of your data, we will remove your personal information from our database, in accordance with the applicable laws and our privacy policy. This includes:

  • Removing your account from our systems.
  • Deleting your personal information, including email, name, and usage data, from our database.
  • Ensuring that no backup of your personal data remains after deletion.

To delete your personal data, you can send an email request to ceo@aiproreach.com. Upon receiving your request, we will proceed with the deletion process and notify you once it's completed.

If you wish to cancel or update your request, you can contact us at the same email.
